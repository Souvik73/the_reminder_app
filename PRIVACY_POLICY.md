This Privacy Policy explains how The Reminder App (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
- **Account and profile data:** If you sign in, we collect your email address and basic profile details such as display name and (for Google sign-in) profile photo URL.
- **Reminder content:** Titles, descriptions, due dates, priority, and other reminder details you create.
- **Alarm data:** Alarm times, recurrence rules, and labels you set.
- **Hydration data:** Logs and daily goals you enter.

### B. Information Collected by Third-Party Services
The App uses third-party services, which may collect data automatically, including:
- **Firebase Authentication and Firestore (Google):** May receive your account identifier, email address, display name, login method, and login timestamps.
- **Google Mobile Ads:** May collect device identifiers (such as advertising ID), IP address, and ad interaction data to serve ads and measure performance.

### C. Device and App Information
We may access certain device information needed for core features, including:
- **Notification permissions and alarm scheduling status** (to deliver reminders and alarms).
- **Device time zone** (used locally to schedule notifications correctly).

We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or precise location.

## 2. How We Use Information
We use information to:
- Provide reminders, alarms, and hydration tracking.
- Enable sign-in and basic account continuity.
- Show advertisements and measure ad performance.
- Improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

## 3. Where Your Data Is Stored
- **On-device storage:** Reminders, alarms, hydration logs, and settings are stored locally on your device using local storage.
- **Cloud storage (if you sign in):** Basic profile data and login events are stored in Firebase/Firestore. Reminders and hydration logs are **not** synced across devices at this time.

Uninstalling the App or clearing app data may erase locally stored information.

## 4. Sharing of Information
We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with:
- **Service providers** that help operate the App, such as Google Firebase and Google Mobile Ads.
- **Legal or safety requests** if required to comply with law or to protect users and the App.

## 5. Advertising
The App displays ads through Google Mobile Ads. Google may use device identifiers and other signals to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your settings. You can control ad personalization in your device settings.

## 6. Permissions
The App may request permissions for:
- **Notifications** (to deliver reminders).
- **Exact alarms and background operation** (to schedule and ring alarms on time).

These permissions are optional but required for core functionality.

## 7. Data Retention
- **Local data** remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- **Cloud data** (if you sign in) is retained while your account is active or as needed for account continuity.

To request deletion of cloud data, contact us at the email listed above.

## 8. Your Choices
You can:
- Use the App without signing in (local-only mode).
- Sign out at any time.
- Disable notifications in device settings.
- Control ad personalization in device settings.
